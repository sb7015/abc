Summary: Who's Best at What?

Best for Tool Integration: MCP (Anthropic) WINNER
Why: Most mature, 300+ pre-built connections, proven in production, easiest to implement
Best for Enterprise Agent Communication: A2A (Google) WINNER
Why: Built for large corporations, comprehensive security, major industry backing
Best for High-Performance Operations: AGP (AGNTCY) WINNER
Why: Fastest response times, handles massive scale, built for complex operations
Best for Privacy & Simplicity: ACP (Linux Foundation) WINNER
Why: Easiest to understand, keeps data private, community-governed, perfect for cross-company collaboration without sharing sensitive data
Best for Future Vision: ANP (Agent Network Protocol) WINNER
Why: Most ambitious decentralized approach, no vendor lock-in, true peer-to-peer
Best Overall for Business Today: MCP (for tools) + A2A (for agents) WINNER
Why: Both are production-ready with strong ecosystems and complement each other perfectly
Best for Privacy-Conscious Organizations: ACP (Linux Foundation) WINNER
Why: Only protocol specifically designed to enable collaboration while guaranteeing data sovereignty and privacy
