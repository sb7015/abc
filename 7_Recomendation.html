💰 Cost Efficiency Calculation

Value Score Formula:

Value Score = (Accuracy × Uptime × Detection Coverage) / (Latency × False Positive Rate × Cost per 1M requests)

Example Calculation:

Guardrail Solution A:

  • Accuracy: 98%
  • Uptime: 99.9%
  • Detection Coverage: 10 threat types
  • Latency: 45ms
  • False Positive Rate: 2%
  • Cost: $50 per 1M requests
Value Score = (98 × 99.9 × 10) / (45 × 2 × 50) = 9,790.2 / 4,500 = 2.18

Interpretation:

  • 🟢 High Value: Score > 2.0
  • 🟡 Medium Value: Score 1.0-2.0
  • 🔴 Low Value: Score < 1.0
