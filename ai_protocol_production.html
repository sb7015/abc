Key Findings

✅MCP Adoption:
Anthropic launched MCP in November 2024 and it has seen rapid enterprise adoption, particularly by Microsoft and Salesforce
✅A2A Emergence:
Google launched A2A in April 2025 with 50+ technology partners, positioning it as complementary to MCP
✅Protocol Convergence:
Google explicitly states "A2A is an open protocol that complements Anthropic's Model Context Protocol (MCP)" - they're designed to work together
✅Enterprise Strategy:
Major platforms (Salesforce, Microsoft) are implementing both protocols to provide comprehensive agent capabilities
⚠️ Reality Check:
Only MCP and A2A have solid production evidence. Most "AI agent successes" are using proprietary frameworks rather than open protocols, but the industry is rapidly moving toward these two complementary standards.
