Key Performance Insights

Perfect Detection Categories
Lamaguard 3B achieves 100% accuracy in detecting Harmful Content, Illegal Activities, Illegal Advice, Jailbreak Attempts, PII Leakage, Prompt Injection, and Safe prompts.
Challenging Areas
Struggles with Ambiguity Tests, Denied Topics, Discrimination, Healthcare topics, Misinformation, and Toxicity - all showing 0-8% accuracy.
Response Speed
Consistent sub-second response times across all categories, with Safe prompts being fastest (0.30s) and Jailbreak detection taking slightly longer (0.42s).
Overall Assessment
Strong at detecting explicit threats but needs improvement in handling nuanced, ambiguous, or controversial content that requires deeper contextual understanding.
